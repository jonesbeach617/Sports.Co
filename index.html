We use the power of sports to fuel therapeutic outcomes. ABAthletes supports children with special needs using evidence-based Applied Behavior Analysis — enhanced by movement, teamwork, and purpose.

About ABAthletes

ABAthletes is a pioneering ABA therapy program that blends traditional behavior analysis with active, sports-inspired environments. Our philosophy: children learn best when they’re engaged, moving, and having fun.

We help children improve:

  • Focus and attention span
  • Social communication and teamwork
  • Emotional regulation and independence
  • Confidence in physical and social settings

Our Programs

  • Mini Movers (Ages 3–6): Foundational behavior building + motor development
  • TeamUp ABA (Ages 6–10): Group games focused on social skills, patience, and teamwork
  • FocusFit (Ages 10–14): Structured fitness routines that support individual behavior goals
